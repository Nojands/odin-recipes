Pozole

Description

Pozole is a typical mexican dish most commonly consumed in the south and center regions of the country, it has different variants but one of the most famous is the Pozole Rojo version, which in my opinion is the most delicious.

Ingredients

  • 5 Liters of water
  • 1/2 Onion sliced to the half
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 3 salt spoons
  • 1 1/2 hominy kilograms
  • 1/2 kilograms of pork rib cut into pieces
  • 1 kilo of pork pulp cut into pieces
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 14 guajillo chilies, seeded and soaked in hot water
  • 2 Ancho Chiles, seeded and soaked in hot water
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 of a piece of onion cut into pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Chile de arbol powder
  • finely chopped onion
  • filleted romaine lettuce
  • Sliced ​​radishes
  • Lemons
  • corn toast
  • 1 Container of refrigerated NESTLÉ® Media Crema (190 g)
  • 100 grams of grated cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pork meat with water, onion, garlic cloves, salt, hominy and the bay leaves in a Express pot for 45 minutes
  2. In a blender machine, include all the chilies with the rest of the garlic cloves and the species.
  3. Mix the sauce and with the cooked porked and leave it with low fire
  4. Serve in a bowl
  5. Add lemon and the chopped onion, filleted lettuce and sliced radishes
  6. Enjoy
